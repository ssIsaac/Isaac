Where-Space

Due to the rising demand for working spaces for students and graduates, Where Space is a platform for users to post working spaces available with ratings of suitability for work, as well as the average noise level, to acommodate to people of different preferences. Additionally, users can organise study sessions, to gather people of a common interest together.

Simplex Method Calculator

Unlike continuous mathematic, discrete mathematic is not as widely taught, yet it plays a crucial role in laying the foundation of computer science. Throughout my years in university, I had multiple modules on discrete mathematic and often when I had to check on the correctness of my answer, there was no online solver that could give me the answer and the steps to achieving it. Therefore, introduce the Simplex Method Calculator, where a step by step working of a simplex method question will be displayed through the use of a tableau.

UniTix

UniTix was developed in collaboration with a client from the King's College London Student Union(KCLSU) as an alternative to EvenBrite. Upon creating an account, users can view upcoming events and purchase event tickets. Admins (society president) have the capability to post club events and monitor the sale of tickets.
